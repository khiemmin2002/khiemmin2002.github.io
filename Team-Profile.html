The Legends of Five

When people work in a group, a name is necessary to have because it will identify the group, besides, it can express the characteristics or abilities of each member. I have already formed a group not only for Assignment 3 but also for other projects later. However, we have not decided on a name for the group yet, in my opinion, I will choose “The Legends of Five”. “Five” will represent 5 members in the group, “Legends” just like a way to reveal what they are good at. Furthermore, everyone can understand the meaning in another way, the name is the combination of each different personality. As a result, the team might become special and unique thanks to the character of the members. Last but not least, the name plays an important role in inspiring not only me but also my teammates a lot.
