My project ideas

Overview

From my previous assignment, AI is the most fascinating part of IT that I would like to figure out. My project is going to be about generating a system with AI integration of using drones to clean windows on skyscrapers. Along with this system, there is a program that will calculate the size of the windows and estimate how many drones does it take to execute the work. AI, as well as machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL), will be applied to the program to ensure the work is optimized. The system will be installed for pilots on some of the buildings located in the city center in Ho Chi Minh City. To be honest, my expectation for this project is really large because it can replace human to clean windows on skyscrapers, thus, abrupt risks would be avoided.  

Motivation

Buildings that are higher than 50 stories appeared more and more in recent years in Ho Chi Minh City. For instance, Landmark 81 opened in 2018 with a total height from the bottom to top is more than 450 meters has become the highest building in Vietnam (Viet Vision Travel, 2018). However, if sanitation workers would like to clean the windows of this skyscraper, it would be a massive challenge for them, especially people that are afraid of height. The purpose of creating a system of drones to scrub the building from the outside is an answer to this problem. The drones will replace workers to do this dangerous task, moreover, all the tasks will be done automatically.

Description

The system for running this service will be known as “Sky Smart Cleaning” or S.S.M. There are many teams with up to 3 drones to do different tasks separately such as flushing, scrubbing, and drying. The reason why the systems need several teams to execute the work is to make sure that the progress will be done on time. Furthermore, workers can always have preventive drones to replace if some of them face technical difficulties. The number of drones could be up to 100 at the same time when operating, which means that the area which needs to be cleaned might be nearly 10000 square meters. It takes 10 minutes for a team to clean with a space of 100 square meters, when we put it into comparison, it is faster many times than cleaning by hand. In addition, the drones can implement the work with skyscrapers which have a sophisticated design. There are 3 types of drones that used, the first one is flushing, this drone has a container that holds water and cleaning fluid. The next is scrubbing, this drone will be equipped with brushes that have a speed of spin at 1000 rounds per minute. The last one is drying, there are two turbines as same as the jet engine are mounted under the drone to create a strong flow of air to dry the windows. Nevertheless, depending on the building, drones that can do two tasks at the same time will be used more favorably to decrease the operating fee although its performance is lower than the one that only does one task separately.

The stages to clean a skyscraper will not be too difficult with the assistant of these drones. First, the owner must send the specifications of the building to the sanitation company within 2 weeks so that engineers can prepare in advance. Second, all the specifications will be put into the program to run the drones, computer will estimate the amount of water, cleaning fluid that is used for cleaning depends on the area the owner want to wipe, the computer also suggests the number of drones to do the work efficiently. Third, the cleaning task would be begun at that building, many groups of drones will be deployed at the same time. The flushing drone will discharge a mixture of water and cleaning fluid with high pressure to the windows, after that, the scrubbing drone will undertake its task, finally, the drone with turbines will dry all the remaining water on the windows. However, if the entire building is not be constructed of glass, AI on the censor of the drone will detect and automatically move the drone to an area that has glass. The work will be carried out from the highest to the lowest level of the building, the work progress will be updated continuously by the AI for engineers and workers. In addition, AI can use the information from the weather to determine a suitable date for cleaning. After finished cleaning, all the data of the process will be stored on Microsoft Azure in order to analyze and reuse for the next time. If the skyscraper does not need to be cleaned entirely, engineers just need to adjust a few necessary metrics according to the customer.

Tools and Technologies

Hardware

Three drones with customized for specialized features: Flushing, scrubbing, drying.
*Notice: We can combine two of the three features above for a drone to reduce the fee.                
                Flushing and drying feature cannot use together due to the problem of electricity. 
A laptop is recommended because this job offers you to move continuously between different buildings, the laptop will be convenient and compact for you. For the specs, you must consider a CPU with high performance because it needs to run tasks related to AI. 

Software

Engineers can develop the program through Visual Code Studio (VCS) with popular languages such as Python, C#, C++, and Java. VCS is completely free so they do not need to pay anything, just download it and start the work. Besides, VCS is open-source, they can easily modify, update the program to be suitable for the work (Open Source).

Skills Required

It can help engineers develop AI in the censor of the drones to detect the area that needs to be cleaned or execute the data of the building. Software

Engineers should have advanced knowledge of Python. They will use this language to develop many tasks related to AI. The reason why Python is highly recommended because the syntax is more understandable and flexible than other languages like C++ and Java. Another reason is machine learning, this is the advantage of Python when making a comparison to C++ and Java, Python is more straightforward and people can easily approach it.

Outcome

This project will be an enormous change for the windows cleaning job. First, instead of spending more than 3 months to clean the outside of a skyscraper, the drones just only need less than 5 hours to complete it. Second, the owner of the building can save a large amount of money using for cleaning the window, the money can be saved up to $5000. Finally, it will be the safest and fastest solution for this job when people do not have to work at a very high place and dangerous. Moreover, throughout this project, I can improve my knowledge as well as skills for developing AI a lot, it will be an impressive experience for me as a software engineer.

Feedback

Giang Nhat Khanh (S3878182)

Vu Le Hoang Nguyen (S3881101)

Both of them are interested in my idea. They said that the drones could help a lot of workers that cleaning windows of high buildings, in addition, it can reduce the number of accidents related to window cleaning on skyscrapers. They were impressed about how AI had been applied to drones so it would operate smarter.

However, there are some problems which I have received in the feedback. The first person is Khanh, he said that all the feature should have been combined together but still keeping the performance or higher. He wants it to reduce more money because the fee to deploy all the drones is too expensive so it must decrease the number of drones. In my opinion, this is a good idea because I can save a lot of money but I think it is difficult to put all the features in the drone. Some problems may incur due to the problem of an electrical circuit in the drone. As a result, it can make the drone explodes or falls during the process.

The next person is Nguyen, who was worried about the accuracy of data of the weather that the drone use, there is no guarantee of full adaptability to unpredictable weather conditions like storms, lightning, heavy sky projectiles like rain droplets or hails. Obviously, I have prepared for this situation, all the drone will have the ability of water-resistant, it allows the drone to withstand water for 30 minutes, in that time, engineers can access the drone through the laptop and order it to quit the task and land. He also mentioned the ethics of automation, workers would lose their jobs due to these drones, these people subsist on low wages and brave dangers to clean windows on skyscrapers. He recommended me to use AR technologies to enable the workers to control the drones from afar, moreover, opening training sessions and workshops on how to operate and maintain the drones will be the best solution to keep their jobs and improve the quality of their work. I think the way of using AR is really interesting, as a consequence, I will open a workshop for the workers to train them for operating the drone with AR, this will create a new job for them and they can have a higher salary.
